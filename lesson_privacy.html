Online privacy

Content is loaded from data/lessons.json.

Key points

  • Limit what you make public on social media and who can see your posts.
  • Only grant app permissions that match what the app actually needs.
  • In group chats with people you do not know well, keep personal details vague.
  • Assume anything you post can be copied or shared; post only what you are comfortable with.
